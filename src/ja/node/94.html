Welcome to SEI-CPP

SEI-CPP has been offering the various seminar for more than 25 years now.
We also offer our internship program for glogal US companies work experiences at their site.
We have over 800 software engineers graduated from our seminar and programs in the past. Many of them are top executives at various companies. We will continue to offer the up-to-date IT information technology seminars. Please join our seminar.

Our university's Computer Information Systems department has been the leader of the IT technology training. CIS department has been selected as designated training site for security from US national security defense and offering International Telecommunication Symposium.

Head line News at our university for CIS department
at Polycentric CIS Earns National Center of Academic Excellence Designation

Cal Poly Pomona once again has cemented its role as a national leader in cyber security education thanks to some recognition from the National Security Agency and the U.S. Department of Homeland Security. The federal agencies recently announced that Cal Poly Pomona's designation as a Center of Academic Excellence in Information Assurance Education has been renewed. The designation in information assurance, also known as cyber security, is based on the curriculum and faculty research in the Computer Information Systems department.
